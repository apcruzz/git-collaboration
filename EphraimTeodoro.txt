Aloha, I am from the Philippines and ill be graduating this fall 2026 as a computer science major.

My project is called Campus Cuts. Campus Cuts is a student-focused platform that connects student barbers with students looking for 
affordable and convenient haircuts on campus. Barbers can create profiles, showcase their work, manage waiting lists, and accept
bookings, while students can browse barbers, join queues, schedule appointments, and leave reviews. Designed specifically 
for college communities, the app helps make the campus barber experience more organized, accessible, and community-driven.
