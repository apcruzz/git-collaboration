I am Hantian Zhang, from China, major in computer science.

For my final project, I plan to build a campus information sharing web application called Campus Connect. 
The app will allow students to log in with a student ID or school account and share useful campus information. 
Students can create posts about selling items, car rental, campus services, map locations, or other school-related information. 
My three main entities will be Users, Posts, and Categories. Users can create posts, and each post can belong to a category so students can find information more easily. 
The backend will store user accounts, post information, and post categories, and the frontend will allow students to browse and create campus posts.
