For my final project I want to build a workout sharing app. I lift a
few times a week and I always end up texting my friends "what did you
do at the gym today" or sending photos of my notes, so I thought it
would be fun to make an actual app for that. Basically Users can make
an account, post the workout they did, and other Users can leave
Comments on it - things like "nice", "how heavy did you go", or just
hyping each other up.

So my three entities are Users, Workouts, and Comments. A User is just
a person with an account. A Workout is one training session - date,
what exercises, sets/reps, maybe a short note. A Comment is something
another User writes on someone else's Workout.

For the first part of the class I'll focus on the backend (storing the
data, login, the API), and then in the last couple weeks I'll add a
web frontend so you can actually use it in the browser. Excited to
see how it turns out.
